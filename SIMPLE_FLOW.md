## Real-World Analogy

Think of it like this:

**Minting an NFT** = Creating a unique trading card
- You provide the artwork (your image)
- You pay a small fee (like printing cost)
- You get a unique card (your NFT)
- It's yours forever!

**Fee Vault** = A community piggy bank
- Everyone's small fees go into it
- When it's full enough, it automatically buys project tokens
- This helps support the project

**Buyback** = Automatic token purchase
- The system takes the collected fees
- Buys project tokens with them
- Sends tokens to treasury (project wallet)
